Holla! I'm Henry Silas, a full-stack web developer. I love web novels so much that I’ve officially earned the God-level title, 'NOVEL JUNKIE.' I practically spend more time reading novels than doing any other recreational activity! I love all genres—except romance, which makes me feel like I’m going to puke. But hey, how many novels are out there without at least a hint of romance? So, I suck it up and read. It’s painful, but I live for this hobby that I love, and I wouldn’t trade it for anything.

Even though most people find it weird when I stare at the screen while smiling—and they often raise an eyebrow in confusion—I’ve gotten that reaction a lot.

So, with regards to writing blogs or articles like this, I’ll continue doing it just for fun. I’m no professional at writing articles or anything, and I won’t focus on a specific niche. I’ll simply be as free as the wind, writing about anything that comes to mind.

So, stick around if you’re interested! If you’d like to get in touch, feel free to visit my contact page for more details.
Wishing you a wonderful day!
